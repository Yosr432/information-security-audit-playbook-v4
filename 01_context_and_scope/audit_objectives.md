## Audit Objectives (Example)

- Evaluate whether information security controls are **designed appropriately** and **operate effectively**.
- Identify gaps that could lead to confidentiality/integrity/availability risks.
- Provide management with prioritized remediation actions and target dates.

Outputs:
- audit scope statement
- risk & control matrix (RCM)
- tested control results (pass/partial/fail)
- report with agreed actions
