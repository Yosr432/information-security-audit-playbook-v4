☐ Internet exposure

☐ VPN / Peering documented  
☐ Firewall enforcement verified  

## Logging
☐ Flow logs enabled  
☐ Retention meets policy  
☐ Logs ingested into SIEM  

## Monitoring
☐ Alerts defined for suspicious traffic  
☐ Alerts reviewed and documented
